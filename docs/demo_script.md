# Sahayak AI 🆘 -- Cinematic Video Storyboard Script

* **Video Pacing**: 3 Minutes
* **Narrative Arc**: Problem → Critical Stakes → Solution → Live App Demo → Future Vision
* **Tone**: Dramatic, empathetic, technically authoritative, cinematic.

---

### [0:00–0:20] SCENE 1: THE OPENING (STORM & STATIC)
*   **Visual**: A pitch-black screen. Drops of rain start hitting a cold lens. Suddenly, visual storm overlay. Shaky, handheld-style video footage of flooded valleys, muddy river banks, and pitch-black cloudy skies.
*   **Audio**: Heavy rain roaring. Distant sirens wailing. The static hiss and crackle of an empty analog radio band.
*   **Voiceover (Calm, deep, narration-tuned)**:
    > "June 2023. Himachal Pradesh. Heavy cloudbursts trigger catastrophic flash floods. Roads swept away. Power grids collapsed. Cellular towers — dead.
    >
    > Forty-seven thousand people cut off. No service. No coordination. No immediate guidance."

---

### [0:20–0:45] SCENE 2: THE CRITICAL FAILURE (NO SERVICE)
*   **Visual**: A close-up of a young man standing in a storm, mud on his clothes. He holds up his smartphone desperately trying to find a signal. The screen zooms into the top-right corner showing a blinking `🚫 No Service` indicator.
*   **Audio**: Wind howling. Panicked voices echoing in the background.
*   **Voiceover**:
    > "In every major natural disaster, the first infrastructure that fails is connectivity.
    >
    > Yet, every modern AI assistant, every digital map, every emergency helpline — they all depend completely on the cloud. The moment connectivity dies… our smartest AI goes completely silent."
*   **On-Screen GFX**: Zooming into mock app screens spinning in loading loops: `"Connection Failed"`, `"Server Unreachable"`, `"Retry in 30s"`.

---

### [0:45–1:15] SCENE 3: THE MOMENT OF CRITICAL NEED (THE HINDI PROMPT)
*   **Visual**: A mother sitting in a makeshift dry shelter holding a young child who has a bleeding arm injury. The screen is dark, lit only by a single phone flash. She presses a button and speaks directly into the microphone in Hindi.
*   **Audio**: Rain tapping on tin roofs. A child whimpering.
*   **Mother (Spoken in Hindi, clear and urgent)**:
    > "मेरे बच्चे को गहरी चोट लगी है। बहता हुआ खून रोकने के लिए मुझे अभी क्या करना चाहिए?"
    >
    > *(Translation: "My child has a deep cut. What should I do right now to stop the bleeding?")*
*   **Visual**: The phone UI responds *instantly* with zero loading lag. A clear, numbered first-aid instruction card appears. The phone starts speaking back to her in calm, reassuring Hindi.
*   **Phone Voice (Synthesized offline Hindi TTS)**:
    > "घबराएं नहीं। सबसे पहले चोट वाली जगह पर साफ कपड़े से लगातार दबाव बनाएं। घाव को हृदय के स्तर से ऊपर उठाएं..."

---

### [1:15–2:15] SCENE 4: UNDER THE HOOD (HOW SAHAYAK WORKS)
*   **Visual**: Cut to a high-end split-screen visualization. On one side, the mobile screen operates. On the other side, glowing neon data blocks fly across a technical flow schematic (merging RAG queries).
*   **Voiceover**:
    > "This is Sahayak AI. A multilingual, completely offline emergency companion running entirely on-device. No internet. No cellular networks. Just local compute.
    >
    > It orchestrates a high-performance 3-step pipeline powered by local Gemma 4 (8B) models:"
*   **On-Screen Text & Kinetic GFX**:
    1.  **CLASSIFICATION**:
        > "Whisper processes local audio. Gemma 4 immediately classifies the category, language, and urgency level of the prompt in under 150 milliseconds."
    2.  **RETRIEVAL (RAG)**:
        > "ChromaDB performs a local semantic vector search over highly dense pre-cached manual indices. It retrieves top chunks from official NDMA guides and trauma first-aid manuals."
    3.  **GENERATION**:
        > "Gemma 4 compiles a contextual prompt. In under 2 seconds, it generates clear, step-by-step action items + localized TTS voice playbacks."

---

### [2:15–2:45] SCENE 5: OFFLINE SPATIAL COORDINATION (OFFLINE MAPS)
*   **Visual**: An operator clicks on a map screen inside the app. Switch from Online satellite map to a high-contrast vector coordinate screen showing pre-loaded grid lines and local shelters.
*   **Voiceover**:
    > "With built-in offline GIS data, Sahayak provides pre-cached maps showing active shelters, trauma wards, and drinking water units.
    >
    > And using peer-to-peer LoRa and Bluetooth LE broadcast networks, Sahayak broadcasts encrypted coordinates to rescue teams nearby — working entirely without GPS data networks."

---

### [2:45–3:00] SCENE 6: THE CONCLUSION & VISION
*   **Visual**: The camera pans up through the dispersing storm clouds. Sunlight starts cutting through. A rescue helicopter arrives in the distance. The mother smiles as rescue workers enter the shelter.
*   **Voiceover**:
    > "In critical times, reliable guidance shouldn't depend on a cellular tower.
    >
    > Sahayak AI brings the power of state-of-the-art AI down to the device in your hand. Democrazing disaster safety. Anytime. Anywhere. Offline."
